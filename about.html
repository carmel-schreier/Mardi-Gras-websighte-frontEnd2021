Mardi Gras New Orleans

Mardi Gras is a massive celebration each year that brings together locals and visitors, the young and old, and everyone in between.
From marching bands to masked revelry, Mardi Gras in the New Orleans area is a cultural event that connects us all.

As Mayor of the great City of New Orleans, I invite you to visit our city and experience the excitement. In fact, bring some friends. After all, it's the biggest party in the world.

LaToya Cantrell
Mayor of New Orleans
